Kayla serves the Order of Odyessa, the goddess of magic. Cassandra is a Ranger, doing the dirty work the Orders turn away from. They never should have crossed paths, but a brutal attack by the god of law on Kayla’s home sends them across Theodyn on a diplomatic mission that looks more and more like the start of a war. To survive what’s coming, they’ll have to trust each other—and face the dangerous spark growing between them.
