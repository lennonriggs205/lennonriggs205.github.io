Lennon Riggs

Lennon is a fantasy writer from Ohio, currently working on Angel, a novel set in a world of gods, monsters, and the mortals caught between them. He also writes Fimbulwinter, a web serial featuring a modern spin on the Norse myth of Ragnarok. When he’s not writing, you can usually find him deep in a story-rich book or game. You can find him on Instagram @lennon_riggs_writes.
